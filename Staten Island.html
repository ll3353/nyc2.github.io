Flag Map Staten Island

Staten Island is one of the five boroughs of New York City in the U.S. state of New York. In the southwest of the city, Staten Island is the southernmost part of both the city and state of New York, with Conference House Park at the southern tip of the island and the state. The borough is separated from New Jersey by the Arthur Kill and the Kill Van Kull, and from the rest of New York by New York Bay. Staten Island is the least populated of the boroughs but is the third-largest in area. Staten Island has been sometimes called "the forgotten borough" by inhabitants who feel neglected by the city government.
